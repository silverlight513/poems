Teitl gweithio: Heddwch

1.
Y siffrydi rhwng y coed a'r gwynt.
Y sŵn o'r adar eistedd ar y canghennau,
Ac y siffrydi o popeth o gwmpas.
Gadewch o'n dod dros i ti.

2.
Anadlwch yn araf a chyson.
Grandwch ar dy calon.

Translation:
The ruslting between the trees and the wind.
The noise of the birds sitting on the branches.
And the harmony of everything around.
Allow it to fall over you.

Breathe slow and steady.
Listen to your heart.
