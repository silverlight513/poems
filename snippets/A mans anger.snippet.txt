A mans anger

How do you
Contain the anger
Of a man
Who wants to punch
A hole in the wall?


More Info:

Started: June 5th 2022
Finished: June 5th 2022

Reason/Description:
My mind was playing tricks on me this day. Anger and frustration had built up in my head and I couldn't figure out how to let it go. I thought about how some people punch holes in walls when they're angry because they have no escape. I try not to be that guy.
