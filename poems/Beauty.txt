Beauty

People forget
beauty isn't what you see,
it's what you feel.

Those sunset skies
of rippled red ribbons
that intoxicate the heart.

Or the smiles
from friends
so lovingly close.

When pure joy
captures your heart.
Blissful ignorance
of the world encompasses.
