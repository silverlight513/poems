I'm not like the others

I am not free, I am not the one who flows.
I cannot ebb with feelings of fantasy
like I see the others do.

These people I see run and dance,
Acting joyful without care or consequence
while I myself sit here to ever endure.

And I wonder to myself why I'm different.
Why do I stop myself copying them,
Is it even me who stops myself?

These risks I see that 
don't appear visible to others.
Are they even a threat, a danger?

I tell myself, I can't risk these choices
so instead I stay steadfast
Repeatedly reinforcing my anxiety.

I'm not sure I can change
But I can make peace,
within myself I can be whole and happy.

I don't need to fit in,
to acclimatise to their lives.
I have my own space to live in.

Living every day inside my mental cage
I do not need to yearn 
for this inimitable life.

I am unquestionably me,
unmistakingly different,
A content unconformist.
